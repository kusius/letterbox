# Account and Data Deletion

Letterbox allows users to sign in using Google Sign-In to access their Gmail account and emails.

## How to delete your account

To delete your account and associated app data, you can:

- Open the app

- Sign out of your Google account

or

Revoke access to Letterbox rom your Google Account settings:

- Visit: Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access

- Remove Letterbox

## What data is deleted

When you delete your account or revoke access:

- All app-specific data (such as preferences or cached email metadata) is deleted from the app

- All OAuth access and refresh tokens are revoked

## What data is not stored

Letterbox does not store Gmail content on external servers.
Email data remains in the user’s Google account and is managed entirely by Google.

## Retention

No personal data is retained after account deletion.
